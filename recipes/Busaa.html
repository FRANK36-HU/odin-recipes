Busaa recipe - Local African brew


Ingredients

  • Corn flour
  • Yeast
  • water
  • solidified cane serum/ sucrose / sugar

    Preparations
  1. Add small amount yeast to corn flour in a can
  2. Pour water into the content little by little to wet
  3. Cover the cun and place it in a dark room to ferment for 36 hours minimum
  4. After the floor has fermented, place the content on a pan
    and heat to drain water content for 20 minutes
  5. The content is again placed in a clean cun, added small
    amount of yeast then water is poured is in high quantity to flood the content
  6. The mixture is left in a dark for 36 hours
  7. Solidified cane serum/ sucrose/ sugar is added three times
    at the interval of six hours
  8. Busaa is ready to serve after after 72 hours
