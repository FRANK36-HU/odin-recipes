Marinated Omena stew with Ugali for 3 people
Ingredients

    For Omena
  • 2 cups of warm water
  • 1 cup of well dried Omena fingerlings
  • Margarine
  • 2 fresh tomatoes
  • Quarter full tea spoon garlic and ginger powder
  • A bulb medium size onion
  • 1/2 cup of milk
  • Quarter spoon of pure ground chilly
  • Quarter full tea spoon fish curry masala and black pepper
  • Quarter full tea spoon full table salt

    • For Ugali
  • 2 cups of maize floor
  • 3 cups of water

Preparation steps

    For Omena
  1. Wash omena fingerlings with wam water in a bowel and drain all the water to air dry
  2. Marinate the dried omena with spontaneously with garlic, ginger,
    fish curry masala, black pepper and lastly ground chilly
  3. Place the marinated omena on a pan and add adequate margarine and heat to fry
  4. After 2 minutes of shallow frying add chopped onion and decelerate
    heat while mixing thoroughly untill the fingerlings hardens
  5. Upon turning brown hard omena, add pieces of chopped tomatoes
    and continue mixing with a cooking stick to evenly balance
  6. Add salt to the stew, add milk while mixing and allow to heat for 2 minutes
    7. The stew is best served while hot

    For Ugali
  7. Boil the water on sufuria until boiling point
  8. Add maize floor and mix the content thoroughly with a cooking stick
  9. Spin the ugali to allow even distribution of heat
  10. Upon the amount of regulated heat with not less than four times
    of spinning, the ugali is ready to be served.
